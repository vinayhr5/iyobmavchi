  • Testimony

I was born and brought up in a lower middle class family at Navapur, Dist Nandurbar, Maharasrtra, India. I want to share my testimony to you all about how I received salvation and how It changed my life forever. So, It began like this..... I had great passion to attend spiritual meetings since my childhood, So every year I used to attend Navapur convention to experince the mighty work of God and even after several years I lost my passion and my interest which I had with my life about my things at that time.

On 2002 when I was around 20 years Old, I was inspired by the worship and preaching in the Navapur convention and I felt it is good to know about Jesus Christ, so I took help of the servant of God to encourage me and Guide me. That is how it was possible for me to study the Word of God. When I informed about it to my parents they did not allowed me to go to Bible College for studies, because of finance problem. Insted, they asked me to leave school and do some labor work. I was very depressed, but i never forgot the passion that I had. So after passing 12th I set out to Udaipur Bible College in Rajasthan even in the midst of the lack of guidance.

On 28th August 2003, I took water baptism in Udaipur Church, and accepted Jesus Christ as my personal Saviour in my life. I had no talents and gifts, but one day Jesus Christ spoke to me through Bible Word which was Psalms '51:17' and he said I want to use you for my kingdom which will be awesome and when I heard the voice of God I became speechless. And this thing I cannot tell to anyone in my family because there was no one in my family who was doing ministry. But God was so faithful that He gave me special talents and gifts which was only for God's glory. Jesus Christ gave me gifts of singing, playing intruments, composing christian music, writing songs worship leading and preach the Word. And when I really understood his work in my life and how it was changing everything in my life, that is how I understood the purpose and calling of God in my life.

Since 2003 and till now I have spiritual fellowship with my Pastor Paul Matthews FILADELFIA FELLOWSHIP CHURCH UDAIPUR RAJ. who helped me growing spiritually and helped me to know Jesus Christ more. And after that I devoted all my life serving for Gods Ministry.
