About Me

Iyob D. Mavchi is a worship Leader and Preacher, Song-writer, Music Composer & Music Album Producer from Udaipur, Rajasthan, India. He is married to Sangeeta Damor and is now a father to little baby Ruhaan Mavchi.

He was Born on 1-6-1983 at Navapur, Maharashtra, India and and is currently settled and doing worship meetings and ministries in Udaipur. He completed his school education from Navapur, also completed his M.A. and He has completed his Theological Qualification (B. Th. & M Div.) from Filadelfia Bible College, Udaipur, Rajasthan.

He is doing worship ministry from 2011. Iyob turned full time musician at the of 24. Ever since, Iyob has penned around 200 worship songs, many of which are sung in churches across India. His songs which are of praise and worship are based on bible verses. His voice is recorded in Various albums of Filadelfia Music, Udaipur.

He has produced 2 albums till now and is always working to release newer albums. His past release was 'Tere Upkaar' 'Tere Vichar Teri Gati'. He also plays instruments such as Guitar, Drums, Keyboard etc. He always travels to new places and new churches for worship meetings and his voice has been a blessing for both the people in rural as well as urban areas of India.

  • His Vision is to preach the Word of God through praise and worship and his Music.
  • He wants everyone in Church to sing and praise together.
  • He wants to spread gospel to churches of India according to Gods plan.
  • He wants to reach the Youths of India and all those people with broken heart and spread the Gospel through his Music.
